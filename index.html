I'm jan Lile, and this is my site.

I've made a few tools to help do toki pona:

  • lipu pi-ilo-pona la
    • sina ken sitelen kepeken sitelen pona
    • sina ken lukin e kon pi nimi sina
    • sina ken alasa e nimi pi toki pona kepeken nimi pi toki [_ijo_nasin_lawa_ike]
  • lipu pi-ilo-ike la
    • sina ken sitelen e nimi kepeken linja lili
    • sina ken ante e nimi pi nasin toki nasa tawa nimi pi nasin pona
  • lipu pi-ilo-nasa la
    • kon pini nena awen   ken monsi-lon nena   pake ijo ken pini   kon awen sijelo awen   nena pini
  • dictionary
    • type words in sitelen pona (heiroglyphics font)
    • look up the definitions of words
    • reverse search toki pona words from english definitions
  • encodings
    • compress & decompress toki pona text
    • convert between an array of tp encodings
    • including emoji, unicode, kanji, & more
  • silly stuff
    • ijo wile ijo,   wile tu-supa awen pake kon   pake tu-supa pini kon seme
